Phone / SMS QR

Create a QR code that opens the phone or text message app.

Privacy first

Generated locally.

The phone number and message stay in your browser. Nothing is sent to Pelaxix, Cloudflare, or a third-party QR service.

Action note

This does not call or send automatically.

Scanning the QR opens the phone or messaging app. The person scanning it still has to confirm the call or send the message themselves.
