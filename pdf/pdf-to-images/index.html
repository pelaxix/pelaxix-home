Privacy first

Files are processed locally.

Your PDF stays in this browser. It is not uploaded to Pelaxix, Cloudflare, or any third-party service.

Supported output

PNG, JPG, or WebP.

PNG is best for quality and text clarity. JPG is usually smaller. WebP is available when your browser supports it.

Page number format

Use commas and ranges.

Write single pages with commas, or use a dash for a range. Spaces are okay. Each selected page becomes one image file.

  • 1, 3, 5 exports pages 1, 3, and 5.
  • 1-4 exports pages 1 through 4.
  • 1-3, 7, 10-12 exports pages 1, 2, 3, 7, 10, 11, and 12.
  • Pages export in the order you type them. 1, 2, 4, 3 keeps page 4 before page 3.
