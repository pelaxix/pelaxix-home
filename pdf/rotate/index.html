Page number format

Use commas and ranges.

Write single pages with commas, or use a dash for a range. Spaces are okay. Only the selected pages will rotate. All other pages stay as they are.

  • 1, 3, 5 rotates pages 1, 3, and 5.
  • 1-4 rotates pages 1 through 4.
  • 1-3, 7, 10-12 rotates pages 1, 2, 3, 7, 10, 11, and 12.
